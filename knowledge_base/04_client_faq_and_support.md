# Client FAQ & Support Guide

Welcome to Vivia Support! We want to ensure that your experience with us is as smooth and productive as possible. Below you will find detailed answers to frequently asked questions, technical specifications, and guides on how to communicate with our team.

---

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

### AI & Training Capabilities
**Q: How does Vivia's AI learn about my business?**
A: You can upload your existing knowledge bases, PDFs, text files, and provide website URLs in the Vivia Dashboard. Our system ingests, vectorizes, and stores this data securely. The AI uses semantic search to fetch relevant information to accurately answer your customers' questions.

**Q: Will the AI ever hallucinate or invent answers?**
A: We employ strict "grounding" techniques. You can configure the AI in your dashboard to only answer based on the provided context. If it doesn't know the answer, it can be programmed to gracefully apologize and escalate the issue to a human.

**Q: Does it support multiple languages?**
A: Yes! On the Pro tier, Vivia automatically detects the user's language and replies in that same language, translating your knowledge base context on the fly.

### Operations & Handoff
**Q: Can I take over a chat from the AI if a customer gets frustrated?**
A: Absolutely. Vivia includes a robust "Human Handoff" feature. If the AI detects negative sentiment, or if the user explicitly types something like "speak to a human," the chat will pause AI responses and alert your live agents in the dashboard.

**Q: How long does it take to integrate Vivia into my platforms?**
A: Most text-based integrations (like the Web Widget or Facebook Messenger) take less than 5 minutes. Voice integrations via VAPI or WhatsApp Business may take slightly longer (15-30 minutes) due to initial setup and verification processes with Meta and VAPI.

### Security & Billing
**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS for data in transit. We **never** use your private conversational data to train public large language models (LLMs).

**Q: What happens if I exceed my chat limits on the Free tier?**
A: The AI will pause responding, and a standard fallback message will inform users that support is temporarily unavailable. You will receive an email notification well before you hit your limit so you can upgrade to Pro.

## 🤝 Coping with Us: Our Support & SLA Workflow

We view our clients as partners. Here is how we ensure your success and manage expectations:

1. **Onboarding & Setup:** 
   Upon subscribing to the Pro Tier, we highly recommend reading the Integration Guides. If you need technical assistance, our support team can guide you through the Meta or VAPI setup processes.
   
2. **Reporting Issues:** 
   If you encounter a bug (e.g., the widget isn't loading, or WhatsApp messages aren't sending), please take a screenshot, note the conversation ID, and send it to our support channels. Detailed reports are processed 50% faster.

3. **Feature Requests:** 
   We are constantly evolving. If you need a feature we don't currently offer (like a specific CRM integration), drop us a message! We maintain a public roadmap based heavily on client feedback.

4. **Service Level Agreement (SLA):** 
   - **Critical Outages (System down):** Acknowledged within 1 hour.
   - **High Priority (Integration broken):** Acknowledged within 4 hours.
   - **General Inquiries (Billing, How-to):** Responded to within 24 hours.
   *(Note: Pro Tier subscribers receive prioritized queuing for all tickets.)*
