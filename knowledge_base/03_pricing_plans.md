# Vivia Pricing Plans & Features

At Vivia, we believe in transparent, value-driven pricing. Whether you are a solo entrepreneur testing the waters or a fast-growing enterprise scaling your operations, we have a plan designed specifically for you. There are no hidden fees, and you can cancel anytime.

---

## 🟢 Free Tier
Perfect for individuals and small businesses looking to experience the power of automated AI customer support with zero financial commitment.

**Cost:** `$0 / month`

**What's Included:**
- **Chat Volume:** Up to 100 automated conversations per month.
- **Channels:** Standard Web Widget integration for your website.
- **Knowledge Base:** Upload up to 3 documents (PDF/Text) to train your AI.
- **User Seats:** 1 Admin seat.
- **Support:** Access to community forums and extensive self-serve documentation.
- **Analytics:** Basic dashboard showing total chats and active users.

*Ideal for testing the waters and understanding how Vivia can fit into your workflow before scaling up.*

---

## 🔵 Pro Tier
Our most popular plan, designed for growing businesses that need robust, multi-channel support, advanced AI customization, and high-volume capacity.

**Cost:** `$49.99 / month`

**Includes everything in Free, plus:**
- **Unlimited Conversations:** Engage with as many customers as you need without hitting chat limits (subject to fair use policy).
- **Omnichannel Support:** Full integration with WhatsApp Business, Facebook Messenger, and VAPI (Voice). Manage all your channels from a single unified inbox.
- **Advanced Training:** Upload up to 100 documents, scrape your entire website, and provide complex rule-based prompts to fine-tune the AI's behavior, personality, and knowledge base.
- **Human Handoff:** Seamlessly route complex or sensitive queries to your live human agents directly through the dashboard.
- **Priority Support:** Get faster response times from the Vivia customer success team via email and WhatsApp.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Deep insights into chat metrics, resolution rates, average handle time (AHT), and customer sentiment analysis.
- **Team Collaboration:** Up to 5 User Seats included. Assign different roles (Admin, Agent, Viewer) to your team members.
- **Multilingual Support:** Automatically detect and respond to customer queries in over 50 languages without any extra configuration.

*Ideal for businesses looking to automate their support pipelines, reduce headcount costs, and scale efficiently.*

## ⚙️ Add-ons and Customization

For businesses on the Pro tier that need just a little extra, we offer seamless add-ons:
- **Extra Seats:** Need more than 5 agents? Add additional seats for just $10/user/month.
- **White-Labeling:** Remove the "Powered by Vivia" branding from your web widget for a flat fee of $20/month.

---

## 🔄 How to Upgrade or Downgrade

Managing your subscription is easy:
1. Log into your Vivia Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing & Subscriptions**.
3. Select the **Pro Tier** and enter your payment details via our secure Stripe checkout.
4. Your account will be upgraded instantly, unlocking all premium features!
5. To downgrade, simply click "Cancel Subscription" before your next billing cycle. You will retain Pro features until the end of the current billing month.

*(Note: Prices are in USD and billed monthly. Annual plans offering a 20% discount will be introduced shortly!)*
